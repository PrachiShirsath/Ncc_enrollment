ABOUT NCC

Unity and Discipline. Leadership and Service. NCC shapes the youth of India.

What is NCC?

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is India's premier youth organization, dedicated to building character, discipline, leadership, and a spirit of adventure among students. Open to school and college students, NCC is a Tri-Services Organization—Army, Navy, and Air Force—committed to developing responsible, patriotic citizens.

Aims of NCC

  • Develop qualities of character, comradeship, discipline, and leadership.
  • Promote a spirit of adventure and selfless service.
  • Motivate youth to serve the nation in all walks of life.
  • Provide an environment to inspire joining the Armed Forces.

Objectives

  • Train youth to become confident, committed, and competent leaders.
  • Enhance awareness and responsibility as citizens.
  • Encourage knowledge, communication skills, and character building.
  • Conduct social and community development activities.
  • Promote adventure and risk-taking abilities.
  • Provide military training and leadership skills.
